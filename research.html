Research

I am a PhD student in Bioinformatics and Statistical Learning Group under the guidance of Dr. George C. Tseng and Dr. Zhao Ren at University of Pittsburgh, my research interests include High-dimensional Statistics, Statistical Genomics and Genetics, Meta-analysis and Subgroup Analysis.

On-Going Projects

  • Optimal detection of weak and sparse signals via adaptive Fisher’s method
  • Optimal detection of signals under multiple scenarios via p-values combination
  • Fast computation of P-values combination methods via importance sampling and cross entropy methods (Joint with Wenjia Wang)

Completed Projects

  • Combining p-values for dependent tests with heavy-tailed distributions
  • Outcome-guided disease subtyping for high-dimensional omics data (Joint with Peng Liu)
